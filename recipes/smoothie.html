Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Description

Why pay $6 for a smoothie?? This smoothie uses ingredients you probably already have at home to give you a refreshing, hydrating, and protein-packed post-workout pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a blender. You can add any powders - whey, cocoa, etc. - you wish to add at this stage, or wait to add them to taste.
  2. Blend on high for ~30 seconds.
  3. Drink fresh, refrigerate for up to 24 hours, or freeze for up to two weeks.
