Caprese Salad

Description

This simple Mediterranean dish is ideal for a hot summer day. The full flavor of mozzarella and burst of tomato are sure to cool you off and fill you up! For extra kick, you can drizzle Balsamic vinegar over the finished product.

Ingredients

  • 3 large heirloom tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 lb fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt & ground black pepper, to taste
  • (Optional) Balsamic vinegar, to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil leaves on a serving plate, alternating between them. Drizzle with olive oil, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
